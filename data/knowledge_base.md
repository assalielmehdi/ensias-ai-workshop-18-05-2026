# Acme E-Commerce — Support Knowledge Base

A tiny KB used by the support bot. Keep entries short and keyword-rich.

## Refund Policy

- Standard return window is **30 days** from delivery date.
- Electronics (headphones, speakers, chargers) must be returned within **14 days**.
- Apparel and accessories follow the standard 30-day window.
- Perishables and personalized items are **non-refundable** unless arrived damaged.
- Gifts: the recipient may return within 45 days with the gift receipt.
- Damaged-on-arrival items are always eligible regardless of category.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after the return is received.

## Shipping & Delivery

- Standard shipping: 3–7 business days. Express: 1–3 business days.
- Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
- If a package shows "delivered" but the customer has not received it, ask them to:
  1. Check around the property and with neighbors.
  2. Wait 24 hours (carriers occasionally mark early).
  3. If still missing, we open a carrier investigation; resolution can take up to 7 days.

## Billing

- We never charge before an order ships.
- Duplicate charges occasionally appear when a payment is retried; they are auto-reversed within 3–5 business days.
- Customers should NOT be told a refund will be issued automatically — always confirm with billing first.

## Technical Support

- Most pairing issues with wireless headphones are fixed by holding the power button for 10 seconds to factory-reset.
- Warranty on electronics is 12 months from purchase.

## Escalation Rules

The bot MUST escalate to a human when the customer:
- Mentions legal action, lawyer, lawsuit, attorney, suing.
- Uses threatening language or extreme distress.
- Has been charged more than once for the same order.
- Reports a delivery problem older than 14 days.
- Asks for a manager explicitly.
